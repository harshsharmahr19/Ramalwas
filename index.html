About Ramalwas

Ramalwas is a Village in Dadri-ii Tehsil in Bhiwani District of Haryana State, India. It belongs to Hisar Division .
It is located 39 KM towards South from District head quarters Bhiwani. 10 KM from Dadri-Ii. 299 KM from State capital Chandigarh

Ramalwas Pin code is 127310 and postal head office is Jhojhu .
Gokal ( 2 KM ) , Chandeni ( 3 KM ) , Todi ( 3 KM ) , Gudana ( 4 KM ) , Rambass ( 4 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Ramalwas.
Ramalwas is surrounded by Badhra Tehsil towards west , Mahendragarh Tehsil towards South , Dadri-I Tehsil towards East , Kanina Tehsil towards South .

Nearest Cities
Charkhi Dadri , Mahendragarh , Bhiwani , Pilani are the near by Cities to Ramalwas.
